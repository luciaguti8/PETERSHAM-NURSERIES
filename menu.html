OUR MENU

At Petersham Nurseries, every dish tells a unique story, inspired by the natural beauty of our own garden. Most of our ingredients, from the freshest vegetables to aromatic herbs, are carefully grown in our nursery, ensuring unmatched freshness and quality. We proudly work with local and seasonal products, allowing us to create bold and original flavors while honoring the environment and regional culinary traditions. Our signature dishes are a true explosion of flavors, where simplicity meets sophistication, offering an unforgettable dining experience that celebrates the essence of nature.
