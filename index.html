Our story

Petersham Nurseries

Petersham Nurseries was born almost by accident when Gael and Francesco Boglione acquired the dilapidated Petersham House in the 1990s. Without imagining it, their new home in Richmond, overlooking a local nursery, would transform into an iconic lifestyle destination, a gardening center, and a restaurant with a prestigious Michelin Green Star.

The Beginning

In 1997, the Bogliones moved from London to the historic Petersham House, an 18th-century property. In front of it, an abandoned nursery inspired them to create something special. Francesco, with memories of his travels as a young hippie in India, and Gael, with her natural elegance and focus on simplicity, began bringing the space to life by integrating their travel experiences and their Italian and Australian cultural roots.

The Evolution of the Space

Over the years, the nursery evolved into a sanctuary blending nature, design, and gastronomy. With a strong commitment to sustainability, ethics, and aesthetics, the project became a destination where every detail reflects the family’s passion for positive living.

The New Generation

Since 2011, Lara Boglione, the eldest daughter of Gael and Francesco, has taken the reins of the business as Managing Director. With an innovative vision, Lara has expanded Petersham’s philosophy, including the opening of Petersham Nurseries in Covent Garden. Additionally, she founded Petersham Cellar with her husband, an Italian wine shop celebrating the family values of tradition and quality.

Today

Petersham Nurseries is not only a place to reconnect with nature but also a symbol of sustainability and slow living, celebrating the beauty of simplicity in the home, the garden, and at the table.

Team

Isabella Thornton; Executive chef

Isabella leads the Petersham Nurseries kitchen with passion and creativity. A specialist in sustainable cuisine, she combines local, seasonal ingredients to craft unique dishes that reflect the spirit of nature. With over 15 years of experience in award-winning restaurants, she is the mastermind behind every menu.

Liam Carter; Head of Front of House

Liam ensures that every guest has an unforgettable dining experience. With his warm and professional approach, he coordinates the waiting staff and creates a welcoming yet elegant atmosphere. His hospitality expertise allows him to anticipate the needs of every diner.

Elena Moretti; Sommelier

Originally from Tuscany, Elena brings a deep knowledge of organic and biodynamic wines. At Petersham Nurseries, she helps guests find the perfect wine pairing for their meals, ensuring that every bottle tells a story as rich as the restaurant's flavors.

Oliver Banks; Sous Chef

As Isabella's right-hand man, Oliver brings energy and technical skill to the kitchen. His specialty is desserts, which he embellishes with edible flowers and aromatic herbs, perfectly embodying the floral and botanical essence of the restaurant.

Sofia Blake; General Manager

Sofia oversees the restaurant’s daily operations, ensuring every detail is impeccable, from the decor to the guest experience. Her focus on sustainability and innovative vision keeps Petersham Nurseries at the forefront of the industry.

Maxwell Reed; Gardener and Ingredient Provider

Maxwell tends the restaurant's garden, cultivating herbs, flowers, and vegetables used in the dishes. His connection to the land ensures the kitchen is always stocked with the freshest, most natural ingredients.
